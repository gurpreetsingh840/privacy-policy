# Gym Lite Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Gym Lite ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

- **Anonymous Usage Data:**
  - The App uses Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, and Performance Monitoring to collect anonymous usage statistics, crash reports, and performance data.
- **Health and Personal Data:**
  - The App sends your age (calculated from date of birth), height, and weight to analytics services when you start exercises. This helps improve app performance and personalize your experience. This data is not linked to your identity and is never sold.
- **Device Information:**
  - We may collect device type, operating system, and app version for analytics and troubleshooting.

## 2. How We Use Information

- To improve app stability, performance, and user experience.
- To identify and fix bugs or crashes.
- To understand general usage patterns (anonymously).

## 3. Data Sharing

- We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties.
- Anonymous analytics and crash data may be processed by Google Firebase services in accordance with their privacy policies.

## 4. User Consent & Control

- You will be asked for consent to collect analytics and crash data on first app launch.
- You may revoke your consent at any time in the app's Settings.

## 5. Data Security
